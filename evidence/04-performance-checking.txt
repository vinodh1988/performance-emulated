Performance Checking Workflow

Start with:
db.serverStatus()
db.currentOp()
mongostat
mongotop
explain("executionStats")

Read these metrics:
- totalDocsExamined
- totalKeysExamined
- nReturned
- executionTimeMillis
- planSummary
- connections.current
- opcounters
- WiredTiger cache usage
- lock wait counters
- namespace read/write time

Important interpretation:
- High docsExamined with low keysExamined usually points to missing or weak indexes.
- High keysExamined can still be expensive if the index is not selective enough.
- High mongotop read time points to hot collections.
- High dirty cache or disk iowait points to write or storage pressure.
